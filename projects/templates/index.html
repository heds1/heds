These are some things that I've been working on. The R Shiny apps are hosted using the free, open-source version of Shiny Server. Pretty much everything is still under development. Please let me know if you find a bug or have any questions!

Aotearoa Commuter Visualiser

R Shiny app

This app was developed as an entry to the Stats NZ There and Back Again data visualisation competition. You can use it to visualise the journeys of Kiwis to and from their places of work, as reported in the 2018 Census.

FI/RE Calculator

R Shiny app

Financial independence/early retirement (FI/RE) is a goal for many people. This app can be used to make projections for your personal FIRE goals. Note that this is in its early stages of development.
